# Distance: Missing or Forgetting ?

I.

I miss.

**I miss people**.

I miss people when  they are away.

I even miss myself when people I love are away.

I can live alone, I don't need them.

But I want them in my life, no matter where they are.

No matter when they come back.

No matter how many times we talk to each other.

Not seeing someone for a long time

means **warmer welcome** when they

come back. Moments of joy,

talking about the past,

experiences and interesting

facts.

**Forgetting feels bad**.

Really bad, like being the only human on Earth.

Like wanting to** fill every corner of yourself with yourself**.

**Ego** leads us when **forgetting people**, **moments** and **feelings.**

**I don't tend to hate**.

But forgetting, regretting and hesitating , yes, they are conditions that

**I DO HATE**.
