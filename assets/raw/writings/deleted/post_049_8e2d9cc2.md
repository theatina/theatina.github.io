# Take a minute to

**Take a minute to** smell the perfume on his clothes

**A minute to** think of the person next to you

**Minute to** get happy for being in that situation

**To** conceive a great idea for the next walk of yours

Sing a song from the deepest of your heart

Look and truly see the world around you

**To** talk about things, from a different view now

**Minute to **create unique memories in your mind

**A minute to** enjoy all that, with every possible sense

In every possible way

**Take that minute**, it is just sixty seconds

Memories, feelings, people, you, worth it
