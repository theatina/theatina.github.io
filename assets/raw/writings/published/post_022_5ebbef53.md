# Life Manual?! Nahhhh

Did anyone tell you how "**right**" looks like?

>
> “-What's good about sad?
>
> -It's happy for deep people.”

Is anybody annoying you with pieces of **advice**, examples or past events others have experienced?

Do you think this is sensible?

You know, living your life according to others' ?

Following the **paths others have chosen**?

In general, is it ok for you to get on the train of another person's life trace and **be just a passenger**?

I Don't Think So.

>
> “When you're a kid, they tell you it's all... grow up. Get a job. Get married. Get a house. Have a kid, and that's it. But the truth is, the world is so much stranger than that. It's so much darker. And so much madder. And so much better.”

Guess what. This is **your moment **in time.

Probably not the only one, as

no one knows what we were before we became what we now are.

It is the time of your choices and dreams.

Of course if you want to **live** and you say you will get in a cage full of **hungry lions**, you should listen to the people saying that this is **not right**. YES. this is not right. you will **die**, so will your moment in time.

However, what you have to do is **not written **anywhere.

Even if it was, you **couldn't **read it now, or probably you **wouldn't want **to . *Spoilers*

Imagine having your **children** around, when you grow **old** and having settled down somewhere. What is the life you would love to **share** with them? What is the example you want to **show** them? How are you going to **motivate** them to live and not give up the "game of life" ?

>
> "We're all stories, in the end."

Build the life you think you are able to** support. **

Manuals are for objects. We are not so simple.

>
> “As we learn about each other, so we learn about ourselves”

No one knows how we "work".

Now that I'm thinking of it, how **selfish** is believing that you can **show others how to live**?

**How** do you know what is right and what is wrong for me?

**How** do you know if this can lead to my destruction ?

**How ?**

You just **don't**.

So I prefer making **my own mistakes **, and be the only one to blame instead of starting to point at others and pretending I'm the victim.

At least **I** chose **my **"fate" and path.

What can be a life manual?

**YOUR **

dreams, desires, hopes, promises, priorities, thoughts, mistakes..
