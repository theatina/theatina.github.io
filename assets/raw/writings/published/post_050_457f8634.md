# And then she killed him 

*He was the one to **fall in love** *

Quickly enough for her to have doubts for her own self.

He was a kind, lovely, wonderful boy.

Inside out

She was a rock, wandering around

Missing things she couldn't have,

Thinking moments that have passed, has been days,  now

Regrets, sadness instead of being in love.

What if, why's that, how come and when, had her **stuck**

****

He created, she destroyed

Beautiful moments that will never come back

Worthwhile walks and talks

Hoping to return

She was not. She wanted to be. Fervently

She just,

 unfortunately, sadly, miserably,

wasn't...
