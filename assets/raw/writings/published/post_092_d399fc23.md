# We all begin as strangers

Every single **relationship** was firstly absolutely nothing.

It is just a step one took. One hasn't hesitated to fall down and they did **move**.

The **end** of it is a step to.

What does count , is the middle of it. The most **interesting** part..

I have to know

**MY** mistakes

**MY** cons

**MY** beauty

**MY** talents

and all the other **MY 's** I can think of.

Then going ahead is a **bike ride**.

**Loving myself**, means being able to love anybody , even any **THING**, or **IDEA**.

I told a friend that thought. He liked it. I **tried** to follow it,** I failed**.

However I will** try again**, who says **failing** is not a** beautiful part of life **?!
