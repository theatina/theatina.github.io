# Everything will be alright 

****

**Everything** will be ok.

Even if it's not,  **something** will be alright.

Even if not,  at least you have a **job**.

Even if not,  at least you have a **bed** to sleep.

If not,  at least you are **healthy**.

Even if not,  at least you have people around you that **love you**.

Even if not, you have,  at least,  people to **give your love** to.

Even if not,  at least you have **water** and **food**.

Do I have to keep talking?

Everything will be fine *in the end.*..

At least,  you have **yourself**

At least,  our **planet** still exists.

Plus, **nature** knows how to **survive**, at least so far ...
