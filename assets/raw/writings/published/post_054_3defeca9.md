# Be proud of it

While in a relationship - friendship, romantic, or whatever you can think of, there are people that hesitate.

Hesitate to say somehting they believe

Hesitate to show their emotions

being afraid of losing someone because of their relationship with another person.

it is weird, isn't it?! think about it..

Loving your friends, your parents, your significant other, means that you respect them, their decisions, their thoughts and way of life they follow.

Why letting one or more people rule your life?

Sometimes , they hide you. They don't say they are talking with you on the phone.

Or when you go out with them, they afterwards pretend they've accidentally met you or even not having met you at all that day...

There are times when they change their whole attitude when others look.

**Act...** like artists but living with the fear of being exposed, I suppose.

***you don't deserve to be hidden, remember that.***

******

You are who you are, a unique YOU , and I don't find a  single reason why someone would want to hide such a personality , a mind , a living miracle.

Some hesitate to tell others about the love and passion they feel for them

Hesitate to be themselves or act naturally

Be afraid of their own spontaneous actions

Be afraid of their own life.

But then you are the one that has to make a decision.

You have people that hesitate, in your life.

People that may have not understood the meaning of true love, that make decisions, only depending on others.

Can you help them?

How can you help them?

Is it good for you? for them?

Perhaps , the only thing you can do, without even trying, is find what you miss from them, and do it for yourself!

They don't seem to care? care yourself for both.

Hiding their feelings? show them yours , twice

And it goes like this....

There is only one question now.

What if your actions collide with their fears?

For example, I want to hug my friend , just because I felt so. This friend , has a girlfriend and she easily gets mad ... and I know it.

So.... what should I do?

My heart goes for the hug.

My mind is hesitating.

***Really, what are we going to be if not ourselves?***

Friends who hide other friends, cousins pretending to hate other cousins, ex- gender-friends not talking to each other because of the new ones, relationships ruined because of someone or something.

Lies

Fake smiles

Where is true love?

And I don't refer to romantic love only

Love in every possible , magical way.

Love to parents, siblings, relatives, ideas, thoughts, places, objects, nature, animals, everything that you can see, hear, taste, smell , touch and feel or think.

This kind of love, when you just let it be and love. sometimes without knowing what that means or how it is like.

That love....

**...you know nothing about, but it feels right.**

******
