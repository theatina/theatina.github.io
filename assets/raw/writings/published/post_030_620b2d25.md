# Someone left today...

*She was breathing heavily.*

*Not only today.*

*..*

**

**

Trying to keep her balance,

failing to do so on the way..

Beeping just to show she's still alive

not for long, though

Today was the day I saw her like this.

She was not even shaking like the last days

Trying to survive? Gone? She's still here but not for long..

Last moments came and I took her in my hand.

Gave her water, what probably she had lack of.

Saw her opening her eyes just once

and then....

She drank a drop of water,

her eyes.. as if she was crying

She died in my hands.

Out of breath, no motion anymore..

one little life flew away, in a tick of the clock..

Simply & quickly, she left , she 's gone.

***She died in my hands***

Sonata left for a place where only free spirits are allowed...

no cells, no bars, no captivity

Someone left today, Sonata left this day..
