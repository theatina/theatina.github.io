# As long as

How can I feel alright here?

Having so much to explore

To see

To visit

People all around the world

Are waiting

Me,  myself..  I'm waiting

To meet

To talk

To feel

Why are we here?

Stable

Settled in just one city for years?

How can we feel satisfied,  completed ?

The world is there to be explored,

Why are we here?

Why do we feel safe here?

Cities

Countries

Languages

Landscapes

Dishes

Drinks

And more

In a way you never knew they would

Ask me a question

Then ask one more

Different from me

It's not the same,  you see?

Maybe they don't speak

The same language as me

However, looks and songs

Melodies, pictures, taste

Worth one thousand words...

Feeling trapped by deadlines

Financial crisis

Future worries

Creating a cage

Then get locked inside

That's how I feel

Appreciate goods in life

But missing something, still

Never knew this feeling

Never thought of that.

A trip was necessary

Or let's say,  more than enough...

To see what you've been looking at

Listen what you heard

Understand what they've told you

Finally walk on the right path.

As long as you are able

As long as you want

While there are still beautiful places

**Keep following your heart.. **

The world is a book

and those who do not travel

read only one page..
