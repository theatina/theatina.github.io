# Weird thoughts on e-paper 

Tv set,  make up, lip gloss

Walking down the road of chaos

Not knowing yourself anymore

Put your lipstick and you're off

To another day of routine

Another person to give in

Confess your love

Hatred or cheat

Being honest to nobody

BUT

Yourself

Don't know where to sleep

Don't know why it hurts

Leaves stopped falling from trees

People with more needs

Shouting,  crying

Laughing or wondering

What you should have done

What you should have not

Regrets and memories

Passions, dreams, hope

Expecting from people

Makes you a living dead

Why wondering?

Why get sad?

Why being let down?

By people who are probably far?

**Why wondering **

**Why get sad...  **

**Why.... **

**By..**
